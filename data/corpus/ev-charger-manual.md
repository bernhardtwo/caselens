# Northwind EV Charger NC-2 Manual

## Specifications
The EV Charger NC-2 delivers up to 7.4 kW over a single-phase 32 amp supply using a Type 2 connector. The attached cable is 5 meters long. The unit carries an IP65 environmental rating for outdoor installation.

## Installation
Install the charger on a dedicated circuit protected by a 40 amp breaker and an appropriate residual current device. Mount the unit between 900 and 1200 millimeters above the ground and follow local electrical code for the supply run.

## Connectivity
The NC-2 connects to home Wi-Fi for scheduling and monitoring. To pair, open the Northwind app, select Add Device, and scan the QR code on the unit. Firmware updates are delivered automatically over the network.

## Troubleshooting
If the charger does not start a session, confirm the vehicle is in a ready state and the connector is fully seated. If the status light is red, power-cycle at the breaker. For repeated network dropouts, move the unit closer to the access point or add a Wi-Fi extender.

## Safety Information
The NC-2 is rated for outdoor use but must not be operated with a damaged cable or connector. The built-in residual current device must trip-test successfully before first use. Disconnect at the breaker before servicing.
