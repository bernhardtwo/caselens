# Northwind Limited Warranty Policy

## Coverage Period
Northwind warrants its products against defects in materials and workmanship for a period that varies by product line. The applicable term and its start date are listed in the Warranty Coverage Matrix and begin on the original date of purchase shown on your receipt. Coverage applies to the original purchaser unless the warranty is transferred under the terms below.

## What Is Covered
This warranty covers failures caused by defects in materials or workmanship when the product is installed, operated, and maintained according to the product manual under normal residential use. Northwind will, at its option, repair the product, replace it with a comparable unit, or refund the purchase price.

## Making a Warranty Claim
To make a claim, contact Northwind support with your proof of purchase and the product serial number. Support will help diagnose the issue and, if the unit is eligible, issue a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Do not return any product without an RMA number; see the RMA and Returns Procedure for details.

## Limitation of Liability
Northwind is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use of or inability to use the product. To the extent permitted by law, total liability under this warranty is limited to the original purchase price of the product.

## Transfer of Warranty
The remaining warranty may be transferred to a subsequent owner within the original coverage term at no cost. To transfer coverage, the new owner must register the product and proof of purchase with Northwind support within thirty days of the ownership change.
