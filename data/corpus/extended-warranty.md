# Northwind Extended Warranty Plans

## Plan Tiers
Northwind offers two extended warranty tiers: Plus and Pro. Plus extends the standard term and adds accidental damage coverage. Pro includes everything in Plus and adds priority on-site service and an advance replacement guarantee.

## Pricing
The Plus plan is billed annually at a flat rate per product category. The Pro plan is billed annually at a higher rate that reflects on-site service. Multi-product households receive a discount when more than two eligible products are enrolled.

## Enrollment
Extended plans may be purchased within ninety days of the original product purchase. Enrollment requires an active standard warranty and a registered product. Coverage begins when the standard warranty term ends.

## Additional Coverage
Beyond the standard warranty, extended plans add accidental damage from handling, on-site diagnosis and repair for the Pro tier, and an extension of the coverage term by up to five additional years. Wear and consumable parts remain excluded.

## Cancellation and Refunds
An extended plan may be cancelled within thirty days of purchase for a full refund if no claim has been made. After thirty days, cancellation provides a prorated refund based on the remaining unused term.
