# Northwind Warranty Coverage Matrix

## Home Battery NB-10
The Home Battery NB-10 carries a ten-year limited warranty on the battery module and enclosure. Northwind additionally guarantees that the battery will retain at least seventy percent of its rated usable capacity for the full ten-year term under normal residential cycling.

## Solar Inverter NS-5
The Solar Inverter NS-5 carries a five-year limited warranty covering the power electronics and chassis. The term can be extended to ten years at no cost by registering the inverter within sixty days of purchase, as described in the inverter manual.

## EV Charger NC-2
The EV Charger NC-2 carries a three-year limited warranty covering the charging unit and the attached cable assembly. Wall connectors installed outdoors are covered for the same term provided they are installed to the published environmental rating.

## Accessories and Cables
Accessories, adapters, and replacement cables sold by Northwind carry a one-year limited warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Items provided free of charge or bundled as promotional extras are covered for ninety days.
