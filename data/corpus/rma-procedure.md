# Northwind RMA and Returns Procedure

## Eligibility
A return is eligible when the product is within its warranty term and Northwind support has confirmed a covered defect. Out-of-warranty units may still be returned for paid repair where parts are available.

## Requesting an RMA Number
Open a support ticket with your proof of purchase, serial number, and a description of the fault. Support will review the request and, for eligible units, issue an RMA number within two business days. The RMA number must be written on the outside of the package.

## Packaging and Shipping
Return the product in its original packaging where possible, including any protective inserts. Remove installed consumables and accessories unless support requests otherwise. Northwind provides a prepaid shipping label for covered claims; the customer is responsible for shipping on out-of-warranty repairs.

## Refunds and Replacements
For confirmed defects, Northwind will provide a replacement unit or a refund of the purchase price at its discretion. Replacements ship once the returned unit is received and inspected, or sooner if an advance replacement is arranged.

## Turnaround Time
Northwind inspects returned units within five business days of receipt and aims to complete resolution, whether repair, replacement, or refund, within ten business days. Customers receive status updates by email at each step.
