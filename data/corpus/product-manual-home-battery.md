# Northwind Home Battery NB-10 Manual

## Specifications
The Home Battery NB-10 has a usable capacity of 10 kWh, a nominal voltage of 48 volts, and a continuous output of 5 kW. It measures 1150 by 750 by 180 millimeters and weighs 114 kilograms. The operating temperature range is minus 10 to 45 degrees Celsius.

## Installation
Installation must be performed by a licensed electrician. Mount the unit on a load-bearing wall using the supplied bracket, keep at least 150 millimeters of clearance on all sides for ventilation, and connect to a dedicated protected circuit. Do not install in a sealed cabinet or below grade where flooding can occur.

## Maintenance
Keep the ventilation path clear and wipe the enclosure with a dry cloth. Install firmware updates when prompted by the Northwind app. Schedule an annual inspection to verify torque on the power terminals and to review the battery health report.

## Troubleshooting
A solid green LED indicates normal operation. A blinking amber LED indicates a charging fault: verify the breaker is on, confirm grid voltage, and power-cycle the unit. A solid red LED indicates a protection event; contact support if it does not clear after one restart.

## Safety Information
Do not submerge the unit or expose it to standing water. In case of fire, do not use water; use a Class C extinguisher and evacuate. Disconnect the unit at the breaker before any service work.
