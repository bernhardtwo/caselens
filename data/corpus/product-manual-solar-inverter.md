# Northwind Solar Inverter NS-5 Manual

## Specifications
The Solar Inverter NS-5 delivers 5 kW of continuous AC output with a peak efficiency of 97.5 percent. It supports two MPPT inputs with a combined maximum DC input of 7.5 kW and an operating voltage window of 120 to 500 volts.

## Installation
Mount the inverter vertically on a shaded exterior or garage wall with 200 millimeters of clearance above and below. Connect the DC strings to the labeled MPPT terminals, then the AC output to a dedicated breaker. Verify polarity before energizing and torque all terminals to the specified values.

## Operating Modes
The NS-5 supports grid-tie, backup, and eco modes. Grid-tie exports surplus generation to the utility. Backup mode powers a protected loads panel during an outage. Eco mode prioritizes self-consumption and charges a connected battery before exporting.

## Troubleshooting
Fault code E01 indicates grid voltage out of range; the inverter resumes automatically when the grid stabilizes. E05 indicates an isolation fault on the DC side; inspect the array wiring. To reset after clearing a fault, hold the mode button for five seconds.

## Warranty Registration
Register the inverter in the Northwind app within sixty days of purchase to extend the standard five-year warranty to ten years at no cost. Registration requires the serial number and proof of purchase.
