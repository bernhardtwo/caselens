# Northwind Warranty Exclusions

## Physical and Cosmetic Damage
Damage caused by drops, impacts, crushing, or abrasion is not covered. Cosmetic wear such as scratches, dents, and discoloration that does not affect function is excluded from warranty coverage.

## Unauthorized Modifications
Opening the enclosure, removing tamper seals, or installing third-party parts or firmware voids the warranty. Repairs attempted by anyone other than Northwind or a Northwind-authorized technician are not covered.

## Environmental and Power Conditions
Damage from water immersion, flooding, fire, lightning, or power surges is excluded unless the product was protected by a surge protection device rated for the installation. Operation outside the published temperature and humidity range is not covered.

## Consumable and Wear Parts
Consumable and wear parts, including air filters, fuses, gaskets, and detachable cables, degrade with normal use and are not covered once installed. These parts may be purchased separately from Northwind support.

## Commercial and Off-Label Use
Products rated for residential use are not covered when deployed in commercial, industrial, or rental settings, or when operated beyond their published duty cycle and load ratings.
